FAQ

1. What is ARTS TOKEN?

It is an internal token of the DAO platform, directed and governed by the community with its own token. Made by and for artists and art lovers.

2. Do I get any rewards from holding ARTS TOKEN?

ARTS TOKEN does not currently provide any direct staking rewards as a result of holding it. There are however indirect rewards that may be reflected in ARTS TOKEN's value. As % of DAO votes, preferred positions on art works, etc.

3. When is the ARTS TOKEN launching?

Private sale starts on 22nd December 2022.

4. How to buy ARTS TOKEN?

On our official website https://artstoken.art/ clicking on “Connect wallet” and next clicking on “Buy token”.
